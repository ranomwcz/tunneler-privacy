Privacy Policy

Tunneler for macOS — Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Tunneler is an offline local multiplayer game for macOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects, which is essentially none.

Data Collection

Tunneler does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking
  • No network connections — the game works entirely offline
  • No accounts or sign-ins required

Local Storage

The app saves your game settings (such as key mappings, audio volume, and display preferences) locally on your device using macOS standard preferences (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Tunneler does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Since Tunneler does not collect any data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: tunneler@example.com
